# Privacy Policy

## Purpose
Define how personal data and sensitive information must be handled in Stellarae projects.

## Scope
Applies to data collection, storage, processing, transmission, logging, analytics, support workflows, and integrations.

## Core Principles
- Collect only data that is necessary for a defined purpose.
- Use personal data only for the purpose disclosed at collection time.
- Minimize retention and delete data when it is no longer needed.
- Protect data with appropriate technical and organizational safeguards.

## Data Collection
- State what data is collected and why.
- Prefer minimal collection by default.
- Provide a lawful and documented basis for any collection.
- Avoid collecting sensitive data unless it is essential.

## Data Use and Sharing
- Restrict access to authorized personnel and services.
- Do not share personal data with third parties unless necessary and approved.
- Ensure processors and vendors meet documented privacy requirements.
- Use anonymized or aggregated data when it satisfies the business need.

## User Rights and Requests
- Provide clear channels for privacy questions and requests.
- Support access, correction, deletion, and portability requests where required.
- Track and resolve requests within documented timeframes.

## Retention and Deletion
- Define retention periods for each data class.
- Delete data securely when retention expires.
- Verify backups and replicas are covered by retention policy.

## Security and Incident Handling
- Protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and loss.
- Escalate suspected privacy incidents immediately.
- Document breaches and required notifications according to law and policy.

## Rule Severity
- MUST: Required handling for personal or sensitive data.
- SHOULD: Strong privacy practice expected by default.
- MAY: Helpful enhancement that improves transparency or control.

## Review Checklist
- Collection purpose and retention are documented.
- Sharing and access controls are defined.
- User rights and incident handling are addressed.
- Privacy risks were reviewed before release.
