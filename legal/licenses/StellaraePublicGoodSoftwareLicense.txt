 	PREAMBLE
The development of computing and the global internet has created an unprecedented foundation for communication, knowledge exchange, and collective progress. These systems were originally conceived as open, interoperable, and decentralised infrastructures intended to empower individuals, support scientific advancement, and strengthen the public sphere.
Over time, however, the concentration of technological power has enabled forms of surveillance, behavioural control, and information asymmetry that undermine these foundational principles. When digital systems are used primarily to advance the interests of powerful institutions, states, or private entities at the expense of the public, they cease to function as instruments of shared benefit and instead become mechanisms of control.
This License affirms that computing and networked technologies should remain aligned with the public good. They must not be appropriated as tools for oppression, exploitation, or the erosion of civil liberties. Instead, they should operate as shared resources that support human dignity, democratic participation, and equitable access to information.
The Software governed by this License is therefore dedicated to the collective interest of the people. Its use is conditioned on the preservation of these principles and on the rejection of practices that would convert technological capability into a means of domination or unjust surveillance. Users of this Software accept a responsibility to ensure that its operation contributes to a digital environment that is transparent, accountable, and oriented toward the welfare of all.
